#Pawfect Match

Pawfect Match is a platform designed to connect pets with their ideal homes. It enables users to discover, adopt, and interact with pet listings while providing shelters and pet owners a streamlined way to manage adoption processes.

##Features
-Pet Discovery
-Browse pets available for adoption with filters such as breed, age, and location.
-Matching System
-Recommends pets based on user preferences to improve adoption success.
-Adoption Requests
-Users can apply to adopt pets directly through the platform.
-User Profiles
-Manage personal details, preferences, and track adoption requests.
-Listings Management
-Shelters and pet owners can create and manage pet listings.
-Search and Filter
-Efficient search functionality to find suitable pets quickly.

##Use Cases
-Individuals looking to adopt pets
-Animal shelters managing adoption listings
-Pet owners seeking responsible adopters
